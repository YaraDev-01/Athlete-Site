Breakfast: Oatmeal with Fruits

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup oats
  • 1 cup milk or water
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/2 cup mixed fruits (bananas, berries, apples)

Preparation:

1. Cook oats with milk/water on medium heat for 5 minutes.
2. Stir in honey.
3. Top with fresh fruits and serve warm.

Alternatives:

- Whole wheat toast with peanut butter and banana.
- Scrambled eggs with whole-grain bread.

Best Time to Eat:

Between 7:00 AM - 9:00 AM for optimal energy boost.
