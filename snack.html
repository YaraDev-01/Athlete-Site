Snack: Greek Yogurt with Nuts

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons almonds or walnuts
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Preparation:

1. Mix Greek yogurt with honey.
2. Sprinkle nuts on top and serve chilled.

Alternatives:

- Cottage cheese with berries.
- Protein smoothie with banana and peanut butter.

Best Time to Eat:

Between 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM or as an evening snack.
